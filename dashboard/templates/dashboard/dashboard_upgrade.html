Frequently Asked Questions

Why should I upgrade my account?

Upgrading your account gives you access to more advanced tools and support, helping you make better investment decisions.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time by contacting support.

Why do I need to upgrade my account before I can withdraw?

Upgrading your account ensures that you have access to the necessary tools and support to manage your investments effectively, including withdrawals.

Can I only change my plan by contacting support?

Yes, to ensure a smooth transition and proper handling of your account, plan changes must be processed through our support team.
